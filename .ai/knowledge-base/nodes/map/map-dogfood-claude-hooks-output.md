The `.claude/hooks/*.mjs` files in this repository are the output of running this project's own `init` command against its working tree. They are regenerable state, not authored source, and exist purely because the repo dogfoods its CLI. They are distinct from `templates/claude/hooks/` (the shipped artifact) even though both come from the same `src/` bundling pipeline.
